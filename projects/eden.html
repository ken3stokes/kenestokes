Eden

A privacy-focused personal finance app that ensures your financial data remains secure through a zero-knowledge architecture.

Project Overview

Eden is a revolutionary personal finance app built with privacy at its core. Using military-grade encryption and a zero-knowledge architecture, Eden ensures your financial data remains completely private and secure. No servers, no data collection - just pure privacy.

Key Features

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

All data is encrypted on your device, ensuring complete privacy

Budget Management

Set and track category-based budgets

Bill Planning

Schedule and manage upcoming bills with due date reminders

Transaction Tracking

Log and categorize transactions

Military-Grade Encryption

AES-256-GCM encryption (coming soon)
